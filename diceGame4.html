Welcome to Yips!

Yips! is the dice-rolling virtual golf game that proves
even the best golfers can hit a shank or two.

Rules:

Hit f12 on your keyboard to open the console and see what your total strokes are for each hole.

No one can cheat! (since the dice are virtual that won't happen)

If comparing your score to a friend's, the lower total amount of strokes after 9 holes wins.

If you hit the ball over the hole, you remain at the same spot and try again until the distane to hole = 0.

Below are the club types to input. Each club represents a dice type, ranging from 20 sided to 4 sided dice.

Clubs

  • Driver: 200 - 10 yards.
  • 4 iron: 120 - 10 yards.
  • Hybrid: 80 - 10 yards.
  • 7 iron: 60 - 10 yards.
  • Wedge: 40 - 10 yards.
  • Putter: 10 - 1 yards.
